Physics Classroom Iframe Extractor

This tool allows you to extract iframe URLs from Physics Classroom interactive simulations.

    Help

    Instructions:

    • Step 1: Enter the URL of a Physics Classroom interactive simulation in the "Enter URL" field.
    • Step 2: Click the "Extract Iframe" button to fetch and extract the Iframe URL.
    • Step 3: The extracted Iframe URL will appear in the text area below.
    • Step 4: Click "Copy to Clipboard" to copy the extracted URL and use it in your own web pages or applications.
    • Step 5: Use the Help button for additional instructions.


    And here's why:

    • 1. The extracted URL is ad-free and fullscreen with no distractions.
    • 2. The extracted URL uses up to 96% less RAM.
    • 3. The extracted URL reduces sustained graphics load by about 10%.

    If you encounter any issues, please ensure the URL is correct and includes 'http://' or 'https://'.
